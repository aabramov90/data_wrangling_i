## %\>%

``` r
litters_df = 
  read_csv("./data/FAS_litters.csv") %>% 
  janitor::clean_names() %>% 
  select(-pups_survive) %>% 
  mutate(wt_gain = gd18_weight - gd0_weight) %>% 
  drop_na(gd0_weight)
```
